================================================================================
TESTING TIPS
================================================================================

1. Order doesn't matter - "how much social media" = "social media how much"
2. The chatbot normalizes input - punctuation is ignored
3. Partial matches work - "social" will find "Social Media Marketing"
4. Multiple services trigger "services-list" view with all matching services
5. Single specific service shows detailed info with recommendations
6. Pricing queries show all 3 pricing plans
7. Project queries show active projects with progress bars

================================================================================
RESPONSE TYPES YOU SHOULD SEE
================================================================================

1. TEXT - Simple text responses (greetings, contact info, not found)
2. SERVICE - Single service with details, price, rating, recommendations
3. SERVICES-LIST - Multiple matching services displayed as cards
4. PROJECT - Active projects with progress bars and status
5. FAQ - FAQ card with question, answer, and category
6. PRICING - All 3 pricing plans with features and popular indicator

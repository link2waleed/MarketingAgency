## Technical Questions (Explained Simply)

### Q: What's a "conversion rate"?
**A**: Percentage of visitors who do what you want:
- **E-commerce**: % who buy = conversion rate
- **Lead generation**: % who fill out form = conversion rate
- **SaaS**: % who sign up free trial = conversion rate
- **Services**: % who book consultation = conversion rate

Better conversion rate = fewer visitors needed for same sales.

### Q: What's "SEO"?
**A**: Making your website show up on Google for relevant searches. Like:
- Someone searches "marketing agency near me"
- Your site appears in results
- They click and visit = free traffic

Takes months but lasts years. Best long-term investment.

### Q: What's "paid ads"?
**A**: Paying to show your ads to relevant people:
- Google Search Ads: Show up above organic results
- Facebook/Instagram: Ads in their feeds
- LinkedIn: Ads to professionals
- TikTok: Ads to younger audience

Pay only when someone clicks. Immediate results.

### Q: What's "organic traffic"?
**A**: Visitors from search engines (Google, etc.) without paying per click:
- Result of good SEO over time
- Almost free traffic (except SEO service fee)
- Best long-term traffic source

### Q: What's "engagement"?
**A**: People interacting with your content:
- Likes, comments, shares
- High engagement = people care about you
- Usually leads to more reaches and sales

### Q: What's ROI?
**A**: "Return on Investment" - did we make more than we spent?
- Spend $1,000 on ads
- Get $5,000 in sales
- ROI = 5:1 (you made 5 times your investment!)

We aim for at least 3:1 ROI. Ideally much higher.

### Q: What's a "lead"?
**A**: Person interested in your service:
- Filled out contact form
- Called you
- Requested quote
- Sent email inquiry

Not always a sale, but potential customer.

### Q: What's "A/B testing"?
**A**: Trying two versions to see which wins:
- Version A headline: "Best Marketing"
- Version B headline: "Grow Your Sales 300%"
- See which gets more clicks
- Use the winner

We constantly test to improve performance.
